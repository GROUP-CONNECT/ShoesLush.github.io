Who are we, and what makes Shoes.Lush special?

Shoes.Lush is not just a footwear brand; it’s a vision brought to life by passionate artisans and forward-thinking innovators. Founded in 2015, we began as a humble startup with one core mission: to revolutionize the way people perceive and experience shoes. Originating in the heart of New York City, where fashion and function collide, Shoes.Lush started as a small boutique aimed at providing high-quality, stylish footwear for individuals who valued comfort without sacrificing style. Since then, we have grown into an internationally recognized brand, thanks to our unwavering commitment to craftsmanship, design, and customer satisfaction.

Our vision at Shoes.Lush is simple: to create shoes that empower people to feel confident and comfortable, wherever they go. We believe that the perfect pair of shoes doesn’t just complement your outfit – it enhances your lifestyle. We are driven by the idea that shoes should be a seamless blend of fashion and functionality, suitable for any occasion and every foot.

At the helm of Shoes.Lush is a dedicated leadership team with a wealth of experience in fashion, design, and business. Our founder, Jaboth Enoch who has worked in the fashion industry for over two decades, with extensive expertise in both high-end luxury footwear and mass-market fashion. His vision is not only about creating shoes but building a global community of individuals who share a love for stylish, comfortable, and durable footwear.

Shoes.Lush is headquartered in New York City, but our reach extends globally through our online platform and international partnerships. We’re proud to offer our customers a range of services including free worldwide shipping, a 30-day returns policy, personalized shoe fittings, and exclusive limited-edition releases. With an emphasis on customer experience and satisfaction, we’re committed to delivering more than just shoes – we deliver a promise of quality, style, and reliability that our customers can trust. Whether you're searching for everyday wear, luxury leather designs, or eco-friendly options, Shoes.Lush is your destination for all things footwear.

Why Choose Shoes.Lush?

At Shoes.Lush, we believe in the power of a great pair of shoes to elevate your look and your mood. By combining cutting-edge design with practical innovation, we ensure that each product is more than just a footwear accessory – it’s a statement.
